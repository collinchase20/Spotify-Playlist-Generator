About

This web app allows you to convert your liked songs from spotify into playlists. There are two options for the playlists generated:

Playlists Generated By Mood:

This option runs a machine learning Support Vector Clustering classifier over the features of your saved Spotify songs to classify them into 1 of 5 categories. (Rap/Hip Hop, Energetic/Dancing, Calm/Romantic, Studying/Thinking, Chill) Each spotify song has features associated with it such as tempo, danceability loudness etc. and the classifier is trained from over 150,000 spotify songs.

Playlists Generated By Popularity:

This option splits your liked songs into 5 playlists based on popularity. Popularity is a value that Spotify calculates for every song, it is a number between 0 and 100 (100 being the most popular). The popularity is based on the number of plays a tracks has and how recent those plays are. Songs played frequently today will have a higher popularity rating than popular songs from the past.
